Such as, Alan Becker who used the program to make awsome animations, and flash was almost the king of the web. But disaster strikes, and websites using flash only have a limited resolution of 1, and flash has many security concerns. Then in 2010, Steve Jobs A.K.A the creator of Iphones and Imacs released his thoughts on flash, and trust me this one ruins flash. Then flash's popularity was going down from 50% to 10%, and in 2017 adobe confirm that flash was shutting down in 2020 becaue of security concerns, and most websites using flash have to switch from flash to other website frameworks, because of security concerns, and slow websites, and trash website quality, and in December 31st, 2020, adobe has launch flash end of life, and most websites have no support to flash anymore.
